**Stephen Sennett** is a cloud technology leader, content creator, educator, and speaker. He worked in the industry for over a decade across in a variety of roles. He holds high-level certifications across multiple technologies, has been recognized as an [AWS Community Builder](https://aws.amazon.com/developer/community/community-builders/), spoken at numerous events, and authors technical content with [A Cloud Guru](https://acloudguru.com/) (a [Pluralsight](https://www.pluralsight.com/) company).

Outside work, he is a dedicated volunteer across numerous organizations, primarily in the Emergency Management sector, serving around the country during several major national disasters.

As a volunteer with the [Australian Red Cross](https://www.redcross.org.au/), he serves as a specialist in Incident Management, deploying as a National Commander, Planning / Intelligence Officer, and Senior Liaison Officer. He is also a member and current President of the [Rotary E-Club of Melbourne](https://rotaryeclubofmelbourne.org.au), as well as serving a Director of the [Royal Victorian Association of Honorary Justices](https://www.rvahj.org.au/).
